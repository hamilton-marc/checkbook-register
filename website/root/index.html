Simplify Your Bookkeeping

EaseLedger is a simple-to-use bookkeeping software tool to allow you to track your business' income and expenses. Unlike most small business accounting software, EaseLedger wasn't designed to eliminate the need for your accountant. Instead, it helps augment your relationship with your accountant by providing you the ability to provide him/her with accurate details about your financial activity.

One of the things you'll benefit from is Easeledger's clean, simple user interface. Much of today's commercial acconting "bloatware" is overly complex and chock full of features that most people don't use (or can't figure out how to use), making it difficult for people to do even some of the simplest tasks.
